About the Studio

Where Art Meets
the Skin

Blessed Tattoo Studio sits at the heart of Houston's prestigious River Oaks district, a place where world-class tattooing is not an aspiration — it's a standard. Since opening our doors at 4701 Richmond Ave, we have built a collective of artists whose skill, vision, and dedication to craft represent the finest the city has to offer. Every piece that leaves this studio carries with it a commitment to excellence that shows in every line.

From bold blackwork and intricate fine line to color realism and everything in between, our resident and featured artists bring a breadth of expertise rarely found under one roof. We welcome walk-ins daily from noon to 8 PM, and we're here seven days a week because great art doesn't keep a Monday-to-Friday schedule. Come in, meet the team, and let's create something extraordinary together.
